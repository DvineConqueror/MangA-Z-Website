About Us Page


The look of this whole website took inspiration from many other websites that provide their users Manga's to read, and entertain themselves.

With a user-friendly interface and easy navigation, our website provides a seamless reading experience. From popular titles to lesser-known gems, our collection is updated regularly, ensuring a fresh selection for our readers. My goal is to create a welcoming community for Manga enthusiasts, where they can explore new titles and share their thoughts with fellow readers.

Henry Dominic


Creator


Hi! My name is Henry Dominic Alicoben TVL12-01, a student of PHINMA - University of Pangasinan. This website includes a homepage with various manga covers and a navigation bar with links to the Home, About, and Contact Us pages.
